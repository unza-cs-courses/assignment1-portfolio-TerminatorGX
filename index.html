About Me

My journey into technology started long before networking and cybersecurity became my main focus. Growing up with a deep love for the arts, film making, and gaming, I was always fascinated by how digital experiences were created and delivered. Movies and storytelling sparked my creativity, while gaming introduced me to the complex systems and technologies that power modern digital platforms. This curiosity gradually expanded into an interest in hacking concepts, system security, and the infrastructure that connects devices and networks across the world. Today, I focus on networking and cybersecurity, with a strong interest in how artificial intelligence can support research, threat detection, and smarter security systems. My goal is to build and protect digital environments that are reliable, efficient, and secure. Web development plays a unique role in tying all these interests together. Through responsive design and modern web technologies, I am able to create well-structured digital platforms that businesses and users can navigate easily and benefit from. What makes my approach unique is that I combine lessons from creative industries like film and storytelling with technical disciplines like networking and cybersecurity, allowing me to design solutions that are both technically sound and creatively engaging.
